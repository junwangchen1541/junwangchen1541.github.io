I am an undergraduate student in Software Engineering at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Ocean University of China. My current interests include deep learning, computer vision, remote sensing intelligence, hyperspectral image classification, spatiotemporal modeling, and Arctic sea ice forecasting.

我是中国海洋大学计算机科学与技术学院软件工程专业 2024 级本科生，关注深度学习、计算机视觉、遥感智能、高光谱影像分类、时空建模与北极海冰预测。

## Research Interests

- **Remote Sensing Intelligence / 遥感智能**: visual representation learning and robust image understanding for earth observation data.
- **Hyperspectral Image Classification / 高光谱影像分类**: spectral-spatial modeling, lightweight networks, and scene classification.
- **Arctic Sea Ice Forecasting / 北极海冰预测**: physics-consistent deep learning methods for sea ice concentration and spatiotemporal dynamics.
- **Mamba and Foundation Models / Mamba 与基础模型**: efficient sequence modeling, vision foundation models, and multimodal remote-sensing systems.

## Current Focus

I am developing research-oriented projects around physically meaningful forecasting and remote-sensing image understanding. I am also building a stronger foundation in PyTorch, scientific computing, algorithmic problem solving, and reproducible research engineering.

近期我正在围绕具有物理意义的海冰预测、遥感影像理解和高光谱分类推进科研工程项目，同时持续积累 PyTorch、科学计算、算法竞赛与可复现实验工程能力。
