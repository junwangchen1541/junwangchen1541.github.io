    Research

    My research interests are still developing, but they currently center on deep learning methods for remote sensing and scientific prediction.

    我的研究兴趣仍在发展中，目前主要围绕遥感智能与科学预测中的深度学习方法。

    Remote Sensing Intelligence / 遥感智能

    I am interested in visual representation learning and robust image understanding for earth observation data, especially where model design must account for spatial structure, limited labels, and domain-specific evaluation.

    我关注面向地球观测数据的视觉表征学习和稳健影像理解，尤其是空间结构、有限标注和领域评价标准共同约束下的模型设计。

    Hyperspectral Image Classification / 高光谱影像分类

    My current reading and implementation work covers spectral-spatial modeling, lightweight networks, and reproducible evaluation for hyperspectral image classification.

    当前阅读和实现内容包括高光谱影像分类中的光谱-空间联合建模、轻量网络和可复现实验评估。

    Arctic Sea Ice Forecasting / 北极海冰预测

    I am exploring physics-consistent forecasting pipelines for Arctic sea ice concentration, with emphasis on spatiotemporal dynamics and scientifically meaningful prediction behavior.

    我正在探索面向北极海冰密集度的物理一致预测流程，重点关注时空演化规律和具有科学意义的预测行为。

    Efficient Architectures / 高效架构

    I am following Mamba-style sequence models, vision foundation models, and multimodal remote-sensing systems as possible directions for future research projects.

    我持续关注 Mamba 类序列模型、视觉基础模型与多模态遥感系统，并将其作为后续科研项目可能展开的方向。
